Overview of the Independent Speaking Task

Task Description

Express your opinion on a given topic.

Provide reasons and examples to support your opinion.

Preparation Time:
15 seconds

Response Time:
45 seconds

Key Skills

Clarity:
Speak clearly and at a steady pace.

Coherence:
Organize your response logically with a clear beginning, middle, and end.

Support:
Use specific reasons and examples to strengthen your opinion.

Common Topics and Sample Prompts


Common Topics

Personal preferences
◦ favorite activities
◦ preferred learning styles

Opinions on everyday issues
◦ technology
◦ education
◦ work-life balance

Choices and decisions
◦ living in a city vs. the countryside
◦ studying alone vs. in a group

Sample Prompts

• Do you prefer studying in the morning or at night? Explain why.

• Which is better, online shopping or shopping in stores? Give reasons for your choice.

• Do you agree or disagree that students should have a part-time job while studying? Provide examples to support your opinion.

Response Structure and Strategy


Introduction:
• Clearly state your opinion.

Example:
I prefer studying in the morning because I am more focused and productive during that time.

Body:
• Provide 1-2 reasons to support your opinion.
• Use specific examples or personal experiences.

Example:
In the morning, my mind is fresh, and I can concentrate better, which helps me complete my tasks more efficiently. For instance, when I study early, I finish assignments faster and retain more information.

Conclusion:
• Summarize your main points.

Example:
Overall, studying in the morning allows me to work more effectively and make the most of my time.

Practice Exercise & Model Response


Exercise:
Respond to the following prompt

Prompt:
Do you prefer to travel alone or with a group? Explain your preference.

Preparation Time:
15 seconds

Speaking Time:
45 seconds

00:00:00:00

Model Answer - Summarized
I prefer to travel alone because it gives me the freedom to explore at my own pace. When I travel alone, I can choose my own itinerary and spend as much time as I want at each destination. For example, during my last trip, I visited several museums and enjoyed the exhibits without feeling rushed. Traveling alone allows me to have a more personalized and flexible experience.

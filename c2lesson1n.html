Welcome to our C2 English Course!


This course is designed to help you master advanced English skills through self-paced learning.

Over the next 20 classes, you'll explore topics that will refine your language abilities, focusing on fluency, precision, and cultural understanding.

Each class will include a mix of theory, practical exercises, and self-assessments to help you measure your progress.

Instructions
Take a moment to familiarize yourself with the course outline.
Understand that each class builds upon the previous one, so regular practice and review are essential.
You’re in control of your learning, so set a pace that works for you.

Course Expectations

What to Expect

Self-assessments to track your improvement.

Reading and writing exercises to enhance your comprehension and expression.

Listening and speaking activities to develop your fluency and pronunciation.

Self-Assessment Exercise

Initial Self-Assessment

1. Rate Your Skills

On a scale of 1 to 5, where 1 means you need significant improvement and 5 means you’re very confident, rate yourself in the following areas:

• Grammar
• Vocabulary
• Pronunciation
• Listening
• Speaking
• Reading
• Writing

2. Identify Strengths and Weaknesses

Write down three areas where you feel strong and three areas where you think you need more practice.

3. Reflect on Past Challenges

Think about a recent experience where you used English.

What was challenging?
How did you overcome it?

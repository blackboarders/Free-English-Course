The Importance of Time Management in IELTS Reading

• Overview of the time constraints:
60 minutes for 3 reading passages

• Common challenges:
Balancing speed with accuracy

• The impact of time management on overall test performance

Time Allocation Strategies

• Suggested timing:
- 20 minutes per passage

• Breaking down time per question type
- 1 minute for multiple-choice
- 1.5 minutes for matching headings

• Adjusting timing based on passage difficulty
- easier passages should take less time

Question Prioritization Techniques

• Identifying question types you find easier and tackling them first

• Recognizing which questions require scanning versus detailed reading

• When to skip difficult questions and return later

Practice Activity

Timed Practice:
Reading Passage

• Distribute a reading passage and set a strict 20-minute timer

• Students answer questions using the time management strategies discussed

• Encourage students to monitor their own timing and adjust as needed

Review and Reflection


Reviewing Time Management Techniques

• Discuss how students managed their time during the practice activity

• Address any difficulties encountered in balancing speed and accuracy

• Provide personalized feedback and tips on improving time management

• Reinforce the importance of practicing under timed conditions regularly

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Avoiding Time Management Pitfalls

• Spending too much time on difficult questions

• Overanalyzing passages and getting stuck on details

• Not leaving time to review answers

Homework Assignment

Timed Practice at Home

• Students to complete a full reading section under timed conditions (60 minutes)

• Encourage them to apply the time management strategies discussed in class

• Bring results to the next class for review and discussion

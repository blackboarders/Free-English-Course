Counting Objects

Exercise:
Look at the pictures and count the objects.
Write the number and the noun.



Examples:
Five apples
Three books
Seven chairs

Plural Nouns

Exercise:
Change these singular nouns to plurals:

1. Book
2. Box
3. Baby

Answers

1. Book - Books
2. Box - Boxes
3. Baby - Babies

Irregular Plurals

Exercise:
Match the singular noun with its irregular plural form.

1. Man
2. Child
3. Foot

a. Feet
b. Men
c. Children

Answers

1. Man ◦ Men
2. Child ◦ Children
3. Foot ◦ Feet

Listening Practice

Now, let's listen to a short dialogue that includes numbers and plurals.

Pay attention to how they are used.



Exercise:
After listening, answer these questions:

1. How many apples do they need?
2. How many boxes are in the car?

Answers

1. Five
2. Three

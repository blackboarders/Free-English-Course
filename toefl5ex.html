Sample Response:

Writing Practice Exercise


Independent Task

Prompt:
Do you agree or disagree with the following statement:
Technology has made our lives more complicated. Use specific reasons and examples to support your answer.

Task:
Write an essay expressing your opinion on the prompt, ensuring clear organization, strong arguments, and relevant examples.

Time:
30 minutes.

Answers and Explanation


Independent Task

Sample Essay

Introduction:
In today’s fast-paced world, technology has become an integral part of our daily lives. However, I agree with the statement that technology has made our lives more complicated.

Body Paragraph 1:
First, the constant connectivity provided by smartphones and social media can lead to information overload and increased stress.

Body Paragraph 2:
Second, while automation has simplified many tasks, it has also led to job insecurity as machines replace human workers.

Conclusion:
In conclusion, while technology offers many benefits, it also introduces new challenges that complicate our lives.

Explanation:
Review the structure, clarity, and examples used in the essay. Highlight the importance of addressing all parts of the prompt.

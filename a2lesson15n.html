Subtitle: Lesson 15

Welcome to our A2 English Course!


Today, we’ll focus on describing people’s appearance and personalities.

You’ll learn common adjectives that will help you describe physical traits and character.

By the end of the lesson, you’ll be able to talk about yourself and others in more detail.

Objectives

• Learn to use adjectives to describe people's appearance and personality.

• Expand vocabulary with common adjectives for describing physical traits and character.

• Practice talking about yourself and describing others.

Vocabulary

Describing Appearance

Examples:

Tall ◦ short ◦ medium height

Blond ◦ brown ◦ black hair

Curly ◦ straight ◦ wavy hair

Blue ◦ green ◦ brown eyes

Vocabulary

Describing Personality

Examples:

Friendly ◦ shy ◦ outgoing

Kind ◦ generous ◦ helpful

Serious ◦ funny ◦ creative

Lazy ◦ hardworking ◦ energetic

These words help paint a picture of what someone is like as a person.

Grammar Focus

Adjective Order in English

Examples:
◦ A tall, blond man with curly hair
◦ A friendly, hardworking woman
◦ A small, green-eyed child

In English, when we use more than one adjective to describe a person, we usually follow a specific order:
◦ opinion
◦ size
◦ age
◦ shape
◦ color
◦ origin
◦ material
◦ and purpose.

Example:

a tall, blond man with curly hair follows this order.

This might seem complex, but with practice, it becomes easier.

Practice

Describing Appearance

Describe the following people:

1. Your best friend.
2. A famous celebrity.
3. Your favorite teacher.

Example:
My best friend is tall with curly brown hair and blue eyes.

Practice

Describing Personality

Describe the personality of:

1. Your sibling or a close friend.
2. Your favorite fictional character.
3. Yourself.

Example:
My sister is very kind and hardworking, but she can also be shy in new situations.

Role-Play

Describing People

In pairs, role-play the following scenarios:

1. You’re introducing your friend to a new person
describe your friend’s appearance and personality.

2. You’re at a job interview
describe yourself and your strengths to the interviewer.

3. You’re talking to a travel agent
describe the kind of people you enjoy traveling with.

Vocabulary Review

b) Full of energy and enthusiasm.

Assessment Overview

C2 English Course Assessment

Objective:
Evaluate advanced language skills in grammar, vocabulary, reading comprehension, writing, listening, and speaking.

Instructions:
Complete each section of the assessment as instructed. This will help you understand your strengths and areas for improvement.

Section 1: Grammar

Instructions: Complete the following sentences using appropriate advanced grammatical structures. Pay attention to complexity, nuance, and accuracy.

Complex Sentences:
Rewrite the following sentence to include a subordinate clause.

1. The company released a new product. It has been well-received.

Inversion:
Rewrite the following sentence using inversion.

2. I had never seen such a beautiful sunset before.

Cleft Sentences:
Transform the following sentence into a cleft sentence for emphasis.

3. The new policy was implemented last month.


Answer Key:

1. The company released a new product, which has been well-received.

2. Never had I seen such a beautiful sunset before.

3. It was last month that the new policy was implemented.

Section 3: Reading Comprehension

Instructions: Read the passage and answer the following questions based on the text.

Passage:
The global landscape of business is continually evolving, driven by technological advancements and shifts in market demands. Companies that adapt quickly to these changes often find themselves at the forefront of their industries, leveraging new technologies to optimize operations and reach a broader audience. However, the challenge lies in anticipating future trends and aligning business strategies accordingly.

Questions:
1. What factors are driving changes in the global business landscape?

2. How do companies benefit from adapting quickly to these changes?

3. What is the main challenge mentioned in the passage?


Answer Key:

1. Technological advancements and shifts in market demands.

2. Companies benefit by finding themselves at the forefront of their industries and reaching a broader audience.

3. The main challenge is anticipating future trends and aligning business strategies.

Section 4: Writing

Instructions: Write a short essay (200-250 words) on the following topic. Use advanced vocabulary and complex sentence structures.

Topic:
Discuss the impact of technology on modern communication and its implications for interpersonal relationships.

Evaluation Criteria:
• Use of advanced vocabulary and grammar.
• Clarity and coherence of arguments.
• Proper structure and organization of the essay.
• Effective use of examples and evidence.

Section 5: Listening

Instructions: Listen to the provided audio clip and answer the following questions based on the content.



Questions:
1. What are two significant ways in which technology has changed interpersonal communication?

2. How has remote work benefited professional collaboration?

3. What are two challenges associated with the constant availability of digital communication?


Answer Key:

1. Technology has made maintaining connections over long distances easier through messaging apps and video calls, but it can also lead to a sense of overwhelm and blur boundaries between work and personal life.

2. Remote work has enabled flexibility and global collaboration, fostering diverse teams and reducing the need for physical office space.

3. Challenges include difficulties in maintaining team cohesion and the risk of isolation.

Instructions: Record yourself responding to the following prompts. Your responses should demonstrate fluency, accuracy, and the use of advanced language.

Prompts:

2. Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of remote work compared to traditional office work.

Evaluation Criteria:
• Fluency and coherence.
• Accuracy of grammar and vocabulary.

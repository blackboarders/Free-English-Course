Welcome to our Academic English Course!


Purpose:
Present research findings clearly.

Tips

Be Clear:
Use tables, figures, and charts.

Be Objective:
Report data without interpretation.

Organize:
Structure results logically.

Exercise:
Create a table or chart for your findings.

Writing the Discussion Section

Purpose:
Interpret results and discuss implications.

Tips

Be Insightful:
Explain meaning and context.

Be Balanced:
Compare with previous research and note limitations.

Exercise:
Draft a discussion interpreting your results.

Preparing and Delivering a Presentation

Preparation

Structure:
Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion.

Slides:
Clear and engaging.

Practice:
Rehearse for clarity and timing.

Delivery

Engage Audience:
Strong opening and clear speech.

Q&A:
Be ready to discuss and answer questions.

Exercise:
Prepare a 5-minute research presentation.

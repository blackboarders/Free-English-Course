Welcome to our A1 English Course!


Today, we'll build on what we learned last time and introduce new topics like daily routines, telling time, and more vocabulary.

Let's get started!

Objectives

• Review greetings and introductions
• Learn vocabulary for daily routines
• Practice telling time

Review of Class 1

Let's start by reviewing some of the things we learned in our last class.

We'll go over
◦ greetings
◦ introductions
◦ and some simple present tense sentences.

Hello, my name is...
I am from...
I eat breakfast at 7 AM.
She reads a book every night.

Exercise:
Pair up and practice these sentences with your partner.

Vocabulary: Daily Routines

Now, let's learn some vocabulary for talking about your daily routine.

Wake up
Get up
Brush teeth

Take a shower
Eat breakfast
Go to work/school

Have lunch
Finish work/school
Eat dinner
Go to bed
Exercise:
Match the routine with the correct picture.

Daily Routine Example Sentences

Here are some example sentences using our new vocabulary.

◦ I wake up at 7 AM.
◦ She brushes her teeth in the morning.
◦ We eat dinner at 6 PM.

Exercise:
Write three sentences about your daily routine using the new vocabulary.

Telling Time

Now, let's learn how to tell time in English.
We'll use
◦ o'clock
◦ quarter past
◦ half past
◦ and quarter to.

1:00 - One o'clock
1:15 - Quarter past one
1:30 - Half past one
1:45 - Quarter to two

Exercise:
Look at the clocks and write the time.

Time Dialogue Practice

Let's practice asking and telling time with a dialogue.

P1: What time is it?

P2: It is half past three.

P1: Thank you!


Exercise:
Practice this dialogue with a partner.

Vocabulary: Food and Drinks

Now, let's learn some vocabulary for food and drinks.

Bread • Rice • Chicken • Salad • Water • Juice • Milk • Tea

Exercise:
Match the food or drink with the correct picture.

Food Preferences

Let's talk about our food preferences using 'like' and 'don't like.

◦ I like pizza.
◦ I don't like broccoli.
◦ She likes juice.
◦ He doesn't like milk.

Exercise:
Write three sentences about the food and drinks you like and don't like.

Practice Conversations

Let's practice having conversations about our daily routines, telling time, and food preferences.

P1: What time do you wake up?

P2: I wake up at 7 o'clock.

P1: What do you eat for breakfast?

P2: I eat bread and drink juice.


Exercise:
Pair up and have a conversation using the example dialogue.

Review and Practice

Let's review what we have learned today and do some practice exercises.

Complete the sentences with the correct words:

1. I ___ (wake up) at 6 AM.
2. She ___ (eat) breakfast at 8 o'clock.
3. They ___ (like) tea.
4. It is ___ (quarter past) nine.

Homework

Great job today! For homework, write a short paragraph about your daily routine.

Include the time you do each activity and what you eat and drink during the day. Practice reading it aloud.

Example:
I wake up at 6 AM. I brush my teeth and take a shower. I eat breakfast at 7 AM. I go to school at 8 AM. I have lunch at 12 PM. I go home at 3 PM. I eat dinner at 7 PM. I go to bed at 10 PM.

Thank you for your participation today!

We will continue learning more in our next class.

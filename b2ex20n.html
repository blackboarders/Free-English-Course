Course Review - Key Grammar Points

Examples for Practice:

1. By this time next year, I __________ (graduate) from university.

2. If I __________ (know) about the meeting, I __________ (attend).

Grammar Quiz:

Multiple-choice questions covering the main grammar points.

1. I __________ English for five years.

Options: have studied ◦ have been studying

2. If I __________ you, I __________ for the job.

Options: was ◦ were , would have applied ◦ would apply

Answers

Examples for Practice:

1. By this time next year, I will have graduated from university.

2. If I had known about the meeting, I would have attended.

Grammar Quiz:

Multiple-choice questions covering the main grammar points.

1. I have been studying English for five years.

Options: have studied ◦ have been studying✓

2. If I were you, I would apply for the job.

Options: was ◦ were✓ , would have applied ◦ would apply✓

Course Review - Vocabulary and Communication Skills

Examples for Practice:

1. A balanced diet is crucial for maintaining __________.

2. I believe that __________ will have a huge impact on our future.

Answers

1. A balanced diet is crucial for maintaining good health.

2. I believe that technology will have a huge impact on our future.

Practice

1. Role-play scenarios based on topics covered in the course

2. Group discussion on a global issue, using learned vocabulary and expressions

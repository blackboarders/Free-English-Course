Welcome to our A1 English Course!


In this course, we will cover basic English skills to help you start communicating effectively.

Let's get started with some introductions.

Objectives

• Understand basic greetings and introductions
• Practice simple present tense

Greetings and Introductions

Let's begin with greetings and introductions.

Here are some common phrases you can use to say hello and introduce yourself.

Hi

Dialogue Practice

Now, let's see these greetings and introductions in action with a short dialogue.

P1: Hello! My name is Anna. What's your name?

P2: Hi, Anna! My name is John. Nice to meet you.

P1: Nice to meet you too, John.


Exercise:
Practice this dialogue with a partner.

Simple Present Tense

We use the simple present tense to talk about
◦ habits,
◦ routines,
◦ and facts.

Let's look at how to form sentences in the simple present tense.

I + base verb
I play
You + base verb
You play
We + base verb
We play
They + base verb
They play
He + base verb + s/es
He plays
She + base verb + s/es
She plays
It + base verb + s/es
It plays



Simple Present Tense Examples

Here are some examples of sentences in the simple present tense.

◦ I eat breakfast at 7 AM.
◦ She reads a book every night.
◦ We go to the park on weekends.
◦ They play soccer after school.

Exercise:
Write three sentences about your daily routine using the simple present tense.

Basic Questions and Answers

Here are some basic questions and answers you can use in daily conversations.

◦ Where are you from? I am from...

Homework

Great job today! For homework, write a short paragraph about yourself.

Include your name, age, where you are from, and what you do. Practice reading it aloud.

Example:
Hello! My name is Anna. I am 25 years old. I am from Brazil. I am a student. I like reading books and playing soccer.
